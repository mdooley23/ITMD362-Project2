What We Stand For?

ISUD is a society of scholars, mostly philosophers, from many parts

of the world, devoted to promoting international dialogue and the discussion

of fundamental questions of universal concern, which affect all humanity, such

as world peace, human rights, dialogical interrelations of diverse cultures, and

preservation of the world ecology.
